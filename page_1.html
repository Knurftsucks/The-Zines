The Lighthouse

OLD

Pulls out another bottle and slams it down in front of YOUNG. is it really even changing it?

OLD

Drink.

YOUNG

Aye, aye, sir.

OLD

In one draft.

YOUNG

You do it.

OLD

GODDAMN YER CALUMNY! The law says ye do as I command! Any word but “aye” be mutiny!

Pause.

YOUNG pulls the cork off. He drinks, and drinks, liquor pouring down the sides of his stubbly face.

OLD (CONT’D)

Atta boy!!

YOUNG

Aye, aye, AYE, AYE! AYE! AAAAAAYYYYYEEE!!!!!!

- LATER

BOTH of them pour booze all over their faces.

THUNDER CLAPS. LIGHTING FLASHES THROUGH THE WINDOW.

- LATER

YOUNG is so drunk, it’s hard to believe. He is soaked in liquor.

OLD has his head on YOUNG’S shoulder. He falls in and out of consciousness.

71.

YOUNG

Thomas.

OLD

Aye?

YOUNG

It’s Thomas.

OLD

Aye.
